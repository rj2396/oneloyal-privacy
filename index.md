# Privacy Policy for OneLoyal

**Last updated: January 20, 2026**

OneLoyal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
* **Account Information:** Name, email, and phone number.
* **Push Notification Data:** We collect device tokens to send you updates about your loyalty points and rewards.
* **Location Data:** If you grant permission, we collect precise location data to identify nearby partner stores.
* **Photos & Media:** We do not request broad access to your photo gallery. We use the system Photo Picker to allow you to select individual images (like profile photos) securely.

## 2. Push Notifications & Your Choices
We use notifications to keep you informed about:
* Real-time rewards updates.
* Personalized offers based on your location.
* Important account security alerts.

**Opt-out:** You can opt-out of notifications at any time through your device's "Settings" menu under OneLoyal > Notifications.

## 3. Why We Collect This Data
* To manage your rewards and calculate points.
* To provide "just-in-time" offers when you are near a partner merchant.
* To improve app performance and security.

## 4. Data Sharing & Security
We do not sell your data. Data is shared only with essential service providers (like Firebase for notifications) or partner merchants to fulfill your rewards. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your info.
